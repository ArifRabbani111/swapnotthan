About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Swapnotthan began as a small group of SUST students wanting to make a difference. Today, we're Sylhet's leading volunteer organization with 200+ active members across three wings.

Our Wings

We operate through three specialized divisions to maximize our impact

Blood Wing

Our blood wing provides emergency blood supplies across Sylhet. In 2023, we facilitated 1,200+ blood donations through:

  • Monthly blood donation camps
  • 24/7 emergency blood network
  • Donor awareness programs

Education Wing

We support underprivileged students through:

  • School supplies distribution
  • After-school tutoring programs
  • Scholarships for meritorious students

Charity Wing

Our relief efforts include:

  • Seasonal clothing drives
  • Emergency disaster response
  • Food packages for struggling families
